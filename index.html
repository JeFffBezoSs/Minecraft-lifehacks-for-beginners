Minecraft lifehacks for beginners

Today I will be telling you 6 tips and tricks if you are a minecraft beginner!

1) The best start in minecraft is to get a wooden axe to get wood and then get 16 wood so you can craft all of your tools later on.
2) A boat is very necessary to craft before a water bucket. As it is onne of the best early-game items
3) After you get your stone tools. You should look for a village. Because there will be an iron golem who will drop 3-5 iron ingots.
4) When you get your first 5 pieces of iron, (from a golem, cave, shipwreck or a buried treasure which can also contain some diamonds inside of it) you should craft a bucket, a shield and a flint and steel.
5) After you got an iron pickaxe and an iron axe - go down in the caves to get more iron, (for a full set of iron armour and tools) 14 obsidian (10 for the nether portal and 4 for the enchantment table) and around 40 diamonds (for full diamond armour and tools + a diamond sword with a jukebox)
6) When you enter the nether get some gold there so you can trade with piglins for pearls and fire resistance pots so you dont die in the nether fortress.

You finished reading my lifehacks! You are better at minecraft now. YAY!
